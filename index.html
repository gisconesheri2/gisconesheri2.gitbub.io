Features

Patient connect at it's core facilitates sharing of medical records between healthcare professionals.

Creating treatment records

The platform allows a healthcare professional to create new treatment records via a series of simple form inputs that would take an average of 5 minutes to fill.

Viewing a patient's record

The patient homepage lists all treatment records linked to the patient. At a glance, the diagnosis as well as dates of treatment can be seen.

Individual treatment records view

A patient or a medical professional can drill down into a single record and access key highlights about a specific visit

Who We Are

Patient Connect is a platform which enables easy sharing and access of medical records by both patients and the medical fraternity

As a pharmacist by training, I have experienced first hand the difficulty in tracing a patient's medical records, especially information pertaining to medications. This problem is especially acute for patients with chronic conditions because of the many encounters they have with healthcare professionals. I felt that as part of the Holberton School Portfolio Project, I could create a way to easily create and share such records in a secure and reliable way that would offer benefits for both healthcare professionals and patients

For Healthcare Professionals

Healthcare professionals usually struggle to access a patient's full medical history because of the fragmented nature our healthcare system and the lack of interconnectedness of the different hospital management systems. Having a platform that allows them to access and add to a patient's medical history would be immensly benefitial in avoiding duplication of efforts and ensuring seamless management of patients.

For Patients

Patient do not normally have access to the details of their medical records. They often have to rely on sometimes inaccurate recollections when they change hospitals and doctors. This often leads to being exposed to similar interventions and treatments with no benefit with a subsequent increase in their healthcare spending and delays in proper referral. By having a platform where an unambigous records can be easily shared, patients can save on time and money and have access to better management.
